# FUTURE_DS_02 – Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn and retention behavior for a subscription-based business using Power BI. The objective was to identify the major factors contributing to customer churn, understand customer lifetime trends, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

The analysis was completed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship Program at Future Interns.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze customer churn behavior
- Identify key retention drivers
- Explore customer lifetime trends
- Understand the impact of customer contracts and services on churn
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Dataset
Dataset used: Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographic information, subscription details, contract information, monthly charges, and churn status.

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation
The following preprocessing steps were performed using Power Query:

- Removed duplicates
- Checked for missing values
- Converted data types
- Renamed columns
- Created calculated measures
- Created churn rate calculations
- Created tenure groups
- Created monthly charge bands

---

## Key Insights
- Customers on month-to-month contracts showed the highest churn rates.
- Customers without Tech Support and Online Security were significantly more likely to churn.
- Higher monthly charges were associated with increased customer churn.
- Retained customers had a much longer average customer lifetime compared to churned customers.
- Early-stage customers were more likely to leave the service.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Improve onboarding for new customers.
- Encourage customers to move to long-term contracts.
- Bundle security and support services into subscription plans.
- Introduce retention campaigns targeting high-risk customers.
- Reassess pricing strategies for customers with high monthly charges.
